HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFESSIONAL VOICEOVER
=======================================

Step 1: Record your voiceover
   - Use ElevenLabs (free): https://elevenlabs.io
   - Or any TTS/recording tool
   - Use the script in: voiceover_script.txt
   - Export as MP3 or WAV

Step 2: Merge with video using ffmpeg
   Run this command (replace YOUR_VOICEOVER.mp3 with your file):

   ffmpeg -i FormSync_Demo_NoAudio.mp4 -i YOUR_VOICEOVER.mp3 \
     -c:v copy -c:a aac -b:a 192k -shortest \
     -movflags +faststart FormSync_Demo_Final.mp4

That's it! The video + your audio will be combined.
